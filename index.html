Video

Hard to live without

***Winning video***
This video is made as part of a campaign made for Alm. Brand insurance company. It was made as a group project and this is a commercial for the insurance product Yndlings. From the 36 videos that was made, Alm. Brand selected 5 that they deemed the best for their product, and posted them on facebook for 1 week each, to see which would get the widest reach. Our video won the competition with these stats:

  • Reached persons: 32.689
  • Views: 19.769
  • Reactions, comments and shares: 296

Tools used:

  • Premier Pro cc
  • After Effects cc
  • Wacom Intuos Drawing board

My role:

I was involved in the idea generation, developing the storyboard and camera work; setting white-balance, and shooting different angles etc.

Images

Splash

This image was created as a splash image for a website about the Arts and Crafts Movement. Because The Arts and Crafts Movement was prominent in the late 19th / early 20th century, I wanted to communicate the style of the time, but also update it and try to make it relevant for today. The image is made in Photoshop, the lady is May Morris, daughter of William Morris, the founder of the Arts and Crafts Movement, and she was also an artist in the movement. I uses brushes that had a similar pattern to William Morris wallpapers to create the effect on the layer mask.

Animation

Don't care about you!!

This animation was a assignment at KEA. The structure of the animation was set, but the story and style was all up to me. I wanted to keep the style simple and fun. That is why I choose very bright colours. The woman is the villain in the story, so I made her features triangled and pointy, the dog and the bird at the heros and are therefore more round and fluffy. It is meant to be a fun game for kids at the age of 8-12.

  • photoshop cc
